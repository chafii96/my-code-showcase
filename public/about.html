About US Postal Tracking

US Postal Tracking is an independent, free USPS package tracking tool that helps millions of Americans track their mail and packages in real time. We are not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the United States Postal Service (USPS).

Our Mission

We believe package tracking should be simple, fast, and accessible. Our mission is to provide the most user-friendly USPS tracking experience available, complemented by expert guides and city-specific delivery information covering 500+ US cities.

What We Offer

  • Real-Time Tracking: Enter any USPS tracking number for instant status updates.
  • City-Specific Guides: Detailed USPS information for 500+ cities including post office hours and delivery tips.
  • Expert Articles: Comprehensive guides on tracking issues, shipping tips, and USPS services.
  • Free Service: All tracking features are completely free with no registration required.

Editorial Standards

All content on US Postal Tracking is researched and written by our editorial team with expertise in USPS services and logistics. We fact-check all information against official USPS sources and update our content regularly to ensure accuracy.
