Understanding USPS Tracking Numbers

Your USPS tracking number format indicates the type of service used for your shipment to or from East Honolulu. Priority Mail numbers typically start with 9400 or 9205 and are 20-22 digits long. If your tracking number does not match these formats, your package may be shipped through a partner carrier.
