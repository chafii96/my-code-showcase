How Long Does "Available for Pickup" Last in San Diego?

For packages in the West Coast region, the "Available for Pickup" status typically updates within 24 to 72 hours. However, during peak shipping seasons such as November through January, processing times at the San Diego Main Post Office can be longer due to increased volume. Packages shipped via Priority Mail usually move through this status faster than those sent via Ground Advantage or Parcel Select. If your package has been showing "Available for Pickup" for more than 5 business days, consider contacting USPS directly.

What to Do When Your Package Shows "Available for Pickup"

Here are the recommended steps for San Diego residents when their package shows this status:

  1. Wait 24-48 hours — Tracking updates are not always instantaneous, especially during busy periods at the San Diego Main Post Office.
  2. Check tracking online — Visit US Postal Tracking and enter your tracking number for the most current status.
  3. Sign up for notifications — USPS Informed Delivery sends automatic email and text alerts for packages headed to your San Diego address.
  4. Contact your local post office — The San Diego Main Post Office can provide additional details about packages in their system.
  5. Call USPS — Reach USPS Customer Service at 1-800-ASK-USPS (1-800-275-8777), Monday-Friday 8 AM to 8:30 PM ET.
  6. File a search request — After 7+ business days without updates, file a Missing Mail search at usps.com/help/missing-mail.htm.
