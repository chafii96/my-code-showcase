USPS Tracking Status Meanings

Pre-Shipment / Label Created

The shipping label has been created but USPS hasn't received the package yet. This is normal — wait 1-3 business days for the first scan. Learn more about Pre-Shipment status.

Accepted / USPS In Possession

USPS has physically received and scanned your package. It's now in the mail stream. Track your package at uspostaltracking.com.

In Transit to Next Facility

Your package is moving between USPS distribution centers. This is the most common status and can last 1-7 days depending on distance. Full guide to In Transit status.

Arrived at USPS Regional Facility

Your package has arrived at a major USPS sorting hub. Processing usually takes 4-24 hours. Check your tracking status.

Out for Delivery

Your package is on the mail carrier's vehicle. Expect delivery today between 8am-8pm. Full guide to Out for Delivery.

Delivered

Your package has been delivered. If you can't find it, check with neighbors and all delivery spots. Package shows delivered but missing? Read this.

Delivery Attempted

The carrier tried to deliver but couldn't complete it. Schedule free redelivery at redelivery.usps.com or pick up at your local post office. Track your package.

USPS Shipping Services Compared

Priority Mail Express

Fastest USPS service. Delivers in 1-2 business days, 7 days a week including holidays. Includes $100 insurance and free tracking. Best for urgent shipments. Track Priority Mail Express.

Priority Mail

Delivers in 1-3 business days. Includes $100 insurance, free tracking, and free packaging. Most popular USPS service for packages. Track Priority Mail packages.

Ground Advantage

Affordable ground shipping, 2-5 business days. Replaced Retail Ground and Parcel Select in 2023. Full tracking included. Best value for packages over 1 lb. Track Ground Advantage packages.

First Class Package Service

For packages under 1 lb. Delivers in 1-5 business days. Most affordable option for lightweight packages. Track First Class packages.

Media Mail

Special rate for books, CDs, DVDs, and educational materials. Delivers in 2-8 business days. Lowest cost option for eligible items. Track Media Mail packages.

Common USPS Tracking Problems & Solutions

Problem: USPS Tracking Not Updating for Days

Solution: This is usually normal. USPS only scans packages at major facilities, not every stop. Wait 24-48 hours. If no update after 7 business days, contact USPS. Full guide: USPS tracking not updating.

Problem: Package Stuck "In Transit" for a Week

Solution: Check if it's a holiday or peak season. File a Missing Mail search at missingmail.usps.com if it's been 7+ business days past expected delivery. Full guide: Package stuck in transit.

Problem: USPS Says Delivered But No Package

Solution: Check all delivery spots, ask neighbors, wait 24 hours. Then file a Missing Mail search. Full guide: Delivered but not received.

Problem: USPS Tracking Number Not Found

Solution: Wait 24 hours after label creation. Verify the number is correct. Try tracking at uspostaltracking.com for better results.
